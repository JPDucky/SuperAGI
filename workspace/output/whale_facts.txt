1. Whales are divided into two main groups: baleen whales and toothed whales.
2. Male humpback whales sing complex songs in winter breeding areas that can last up to 20 minutes.
3. The blue whale is the largest animal that ever lived, growing up to 90 feet and weighing as much as 24 elephants.
4. Bowhead whales can live for more than 200 years, and killer whales can live for more than 100 years.
